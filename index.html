Steps to Follow:

  1. Mix the Powder with Water – Take the required amount and mix it with water or rose water to form a smooth paste.
  2. Apply on the Skin – Spread the paste evenly over the area with unwanted hair.
  3. Leave for 5 Minutes – Let the mixture sit and work on the hair.
  4. Wipe with a Wet Cloth – Use a damp cloth to gently wipe off the paste along with the hair.
  5. Rinse and Moisturize – Wash the area and apply aloe vera or coconut oil.
